About Rivalry SMP

Rivalry SMP is a hardcore Lifesteal Minecraft server where every fight matters. Players steal hearts from each other to survive and dominate. Recommended Minecraft version: 1.21.10 (any Java version works).

Server Rules

  • Do not use exploits or cheats (including unfair resource packs, mods, etc.).
  • No rudeness, racism, toxicity, or harassment.
  • No alt accounts.
  • Do not harass or annoy staff.
  • No pornographic content (will result in a ban).
  • Moderation actions are final. Open a ticket if you feel unfairly punished.
  • No spawn killing (killing a player multiple times at their spawn).
  • No spamming.
  • Impersonating staff is prohibited.
  • If staff tells you to stop, comply immediately.
  • No combat logging.
  • No bug abusing (string dupers allowed).
  • No advertising.
  • Do not request items back after PvP fights.
  • Do not kill unarmed players (unless attacked).
  • Naked killing rule: you cannot give someone armor to bypass the system.
  • Mace holders must be active at least 1 hour daily.
  • Use common sense!
  • Respect all rules; do not try to bypass them.
  • Team cap per team: 8 players.
  • Read all rules in the dedicated rules channel.
  • Saying “admin abuse” on the owner is not prohibited but can result in punishment if rude.
  • Griefing spawn is not allowed.
  • CPvP / Anchor PvP is forbidden and will result in a ban.
  • No threatening or blackmailing anyone.
  • Unfair actions are punishable.

These rules may change at any time. Please review them before joining.
